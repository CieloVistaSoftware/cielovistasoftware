## POTENTIAL ISSUES & SOLUTIONS

### Low Initial Engagement (< 10 comments in first hour)
- Post provocative comment with question
- Tag 2-3 relevant connections
- Share more aggressively to story
- Repost pull quote at 2-hour mark

### Negative/Hostile Comments
- Stay professional and curious
- Ask clarifying questions
- Acknowledge valid points
- Redirect to data/methodology

### "SYSTEMATIC" Comments Overwhelming
- Set up auto-responder with Google Form link
- Create DM template for efficient response
- Consider assistant help for follow-up

### Technical Errors in Article Pointed Out
- Thank commenter publicly
- Correct immediately
- Update article
- Post correction comment
